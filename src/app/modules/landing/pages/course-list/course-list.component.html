Information and Communications Technology

Designed to provide a broad understanding of information and communication technology, our BSIT program delivers an innovative education that equips students with practical knowledge and real-life skills to adapt in a rapidly evolving ICT-driven world. Grasp various technical expertise from cloud computing, mobile & web development, cybersecurity, user experience design to information management, and get industry-standard certifications from our global partners including Huawei, Microsoft, and Oracle to name a few.

Skills learned

  • Cloud Computing
  • Data Science and Analytics
  • Cybersecurity
  • Mobile and Web Development

Career Oppurtunities

  • Application Developer
  • Client Support Specialist
  • Cloud Architect
  • Cybersecurity Specialist

Business Management

Committed to instill an extensive understanding in the business field, our BSBA program provides quality entrepreneurship education, along with its comprehensive curriculum, that develops business-driven character among students that will allow them to thrive in the ever-changing industry. Learn all about the principles and conventions associated with the adaptive style of managing a business from basic finance and economics, marketing, and customer service, among other courses that are essential in running an effective business.

Skills learned

  • Business Management
  • Marketing, Sales, and Customer Service
  • Operations Management/li>
  • Entrepreneurial Management

Career Oppurtunities

  • Marketing Associate/Manager
  • General and Operations Manager
  • Logistics/facilities Manager
  • Business Developer/Entrepreneur
